Internet Protocol television(IPTV)

International IPTV Service

Aynascope IPTV (Internet Protocol television) is a service that provides television programming and other video content using the Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol (TCP/IP) suite, as opposed to broadcast TV, cable TV or satellite signals.

Aynascope IPTV service, typically distributed by a service provider, delivers live TV programs or on-demand video content via IP networks.

Subscription-free digital channel's platform that stream ad-supported content via Connected TV devices.
